## The Five Signal Model
AI platforms form confidence through five signal layers:
1. Identity Clarity (22%) - Can AI describe the business consistently?
2. Subject Authority (28%) - Does it demonstrate specific expertise?
3. Meaning Architecture (25%) - Can AI read and extract from the site?
4. Ecosystem Validation (15%) - Do independent sources confirm the site?
5. Signal Consistency (10%) - Is the description consistent over time?
