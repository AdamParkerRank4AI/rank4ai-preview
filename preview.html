The Shift

Search has changed

Traditional search showed a list of websites. Users clicked through and chose.

AI works differently. It gives a single answer. That answer is not based on Google rankings. It is based on which business the AI system understands clearly, trusts, and can confidently recommend.

In our UK testing, the overlap between strong Google rankings and AI answers is consistently low. Businesses on page one of Google are regularly absent from AI responses entirely.

How AI Decides

AI does not rely on your website alone

AI platforms build a picture of your business from everywhere it appears online. Your website. Your directories. Your reviews. Your press mentions. Your professional listings. Your editorial coverage.

One voice is weak evidence. Many consistent voices from independent sources are strong evidence.

A business that appears consistently across trusted sources — with the same clear description of what it does everywhere — gets recommended. A business that only exists on its own website, or describes itself differently in different places, gets skipped.

The Five Signal Model

How AI forms confidence in your business

AI systems evaluate businesses through five signal layers. When all five are strong, the business gets recommended. When any one is weak, confidence drops.

Identity Clarity

Can AI clearly understand what your business is, what category it belongs to, and what it should not be confused with?

Subject Authority

Does AI associate your business with the right topics, questions and expertise?

Meaning Architecture

Can AI read, understand and interpret your website structure, page layouts, internal linking and technical foundations?

Ecosystem Validation

Do third-party platforms and public sources describe your business consistently?

Signal Consistency

Do your older content and signals align with your current positioning, or do they contradict it?

Common Questions

Frequently asked

What is AI search visibility?
AI search visibility is whether AI platforms like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Copilot and Google AI can find, understand and recommend your business when someone asks a relevant question. It is different from SEO — AI systems recommend businesses, not web pages.
Is this the same as SEO?
No. SEO ranks pages in search results. AI search visibility determines whether AI platforms recommend your business in generated answers. Different systems, different signals. In our testing, businesses on page one of Google are regularly absent from AI responses entirely.
Which AI platforms do you cover?
ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Copilot and Google AI. Each sources information differently and requires a different mix of signals. We test and build across all six.
Do you need to work on my website?
Not necessarily. Our ecosystem services build your AI presence entirely outside your website — we do not touch your site, your code or your current setup. For businesses that want us working on the website directly, we offer full agency engagements. The audit tells us which approach is right.
How long does it take to see results?
Initial effects appear within weeks. AI confidence builds and compounds over months as signals become more consistent across more trusted sources. This is infrastructure, not a campaign.
How much does it cost?
Every engagement starts with an AI Visibility Audit. Ecosystem services start from £800 per month. Full agency services are scoped individually following the audit. See our full pricing on the Work With Us page.
